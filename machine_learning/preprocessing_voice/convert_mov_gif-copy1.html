    total time of audio

    12 Apr 2020

    Calculate the total time of all audio files at a location.

    EPOCH='jan 1 1970'; sum=0; for i in `soxi * | grep Duration | awk -F" " '{print $3}' | grep :`; do sum="$(date -u -d "$EPOCH $i" +%s) + $sum"; done; echo "total = `echo $sum | bc` seconds"
